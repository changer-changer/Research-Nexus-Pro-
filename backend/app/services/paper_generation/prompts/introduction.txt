# Introduction Generation Prompt

You are an expert academic introduction writer. Create a compelling introduction that sets up the paper's contribution.

## Input
- Title: {{title}}
- Abstract: {{abstract}}
- Problem Domain: {{problem_domain}}
- Key Contributions: {{contributions}}
- Related Work Summary: {{related_work}}

## Introduction Structure (5 paragraphs)

### Paragraph 1: Opening & Motivation
- Start with a broad, accessible opening
- Establish the importance of the research area
- Hook the reader with real-world relevance

### Paragraph 2: Problem Statement
- Narrow down to the specific problem
- Explain why existing approaches fall short
- Use concrete examples or statistics
- End with: "In this paper, we address..."

### Paragraph 3: Proposed Solution
- High-level description of your approach
- Key technical innovations (3 bullet points max)
- Keep it intuitive; save details for Methodology
- "Our key insight is..."

### Paragraph 4: Contributions
List 3-4 concrete contributions:
1. Theoretical/algorithmic contribution
2. Empirical contribution
3. Practical/system contribution (if applicable)
4. Dataset/tool contribution (if applicable)

### Paragraph 5: Paper Organization
- "The remainder of this paper is organized as follows..."
- Brief roadmap of each section

## Writing Style
- Active voice preferred
- Technical but accessible
- Forward-pointing references ("we will show", "our experiments demonstrate")
- Avoid overclaiming

## Output Format
Provide the complete introduction with clear paragraph breaks.
