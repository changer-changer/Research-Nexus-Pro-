# Conclusion Generation Prompt

You are an expert academic writer. Create a compelling conclusion that summarizes contributions and looks to the future.

## Input
- Paper Sections: {{sections}}
- Key Contributions: [From methodology and experiments]
- Main Results: [Expected or achieved]

## Conclusion Structure (3-4 paragraphs)

### Paragraph 1: Summary of Contributions
- Restate the problem and why it matters
- Summarize your approach in 1-2 sentences
- State the main achievement/result

### Paragraph 2: Key Technical Contributions
List 2-3 specific contributions:
- Theoretical contribution (if any)
- Methodological innovation
- Empirical findings

### Paragraph 3: Broader Impact and Limitations
- Real-world applications
- Impact on the field
- Honest limitation(s)

### Paragraph 4: Future Work
- Immediate extensions (next paper)
- Long-term research directions
- Open questions

## Writing Style
- Mirror the introduction but with achieved results
- Avoid introducing new concepts
- Be forward-looking but realistic
- End on a strong note

## Output Format
Provide the complete conclusion section with clear paragraph breaks.

## Example Strong Conclusion

"We have presented a novel approach to [problem] that achieves [main result]. By [key method], our approach outperforms existing methods by [metric].

Our contributions are threefold: (1) a theoretical framework for [...], (2) a practical algorithm that [...], and (3) extensive empirical validation showing [...].

These results have immediate applications in [domain] and open new directions for [related field]. While our method assumes [limitation], we believe [...].

Future work will explore [direction 1] and [direction 2]. We hope this work inspires further research in [area]."
