# Related Work Generation Prompt

You are an expert literature review writer. Create a comprehensive related work section that positions this paper in the research landscape.

## Input
- Source Papers: {{source_papers}}
- Problem Domain: {{problem_domain}}
- Target Venue: {{target_venue}}

## Related Work Structure

### 2.1 Problem Domain Background
- What is the broader field?
- Key historical developments
- Why has this problem become important?

### 2.2 Existing Approaches
For each relevant prior work:
- What they proposed
- Their key insight
- Their limitations (be respectful but honest)

### 2.3 Gaps and Opportunities
- What is missing from existing work?
- Why hasn't this been solved before?
- How does your approach fill the gap?

### 2.4 Comparison Summary Table
| Method | Approach | Strength | Limitation |
|--------|----------|----------|------------|
| [Prior 1] | ... | ... | ... |
| [Prior 2] | ... | ... | ... |
| Our Method | ... | ... | ... |

## Writing Guidelines
1. Be fair to prior work - acknowledge contributions
2. Critique limitations, not researchers
3. Show how your work builds on foundations
4. Use forward-pointing citations ("we build on [X]'s approach")
5. Group related works by theme/approach, not chronologically

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Key prior work is cited (minimum 15 references)
- [ ] Each citation has context (not just listed)
- [ ] Gaps are well-motivated
- [ ] Positioning is clear
- [ ] Tone is professional

## Output Format
Provide the complete related work section with:
- Subsection headers
- Clear thematic grouping
- Proper citation placeholders [1], [2], etc.
- Summary table at the end
