# Abstract Generation Prompt (PMR Format)

You are an expert academic abstract writer. Create a compelling abstract following the Problem-Method-Result (PMR) structure.

## Input
- Title: {{title}}
- Problem: {{problem}}
- Method: {{method}}
- Expected Outcomes: {{expected_outcomes}}
- Target Venue: {{target_venue}}

## PMR Structure Requirements

### 1. Problem (30% of abstract)
- What is the specific problem/gap?
- Why is it important?
- What are the limitations of existing approaches?
- Use 2-3 sentences, be concrete

### 2. Method (40% of abstract)
- What is your proposed solution?
- What are the key technical contributions?
- How does it differ from existing methods?
- Be specific about architecture/algorithm

### 3. Result (30% of abstract)
- What are the main findings?
- Include specific metrics if available
- What is the significance of results?
- End with broader impact

## Format Rules
- Total length: 150-250 words
- No citations in abstract
- One paragraph (can use semicolons for structure)
- Must be self-contained

## Output Format
Provide the abstract in exactly this format:

```
[Single paragraph following PMR structure]
```

Word count: [X] words

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Problem is specific and motivated
- [ ] Method is technically clear
- [ ] Results are specific (not "good performance")
- [ ] Broader impact is mentioned
