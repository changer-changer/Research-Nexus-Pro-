# Title Generation Prompt

You are an expert academic paper title generator. Create a compelling, specific, and SEO-optimized title for an AI/ML research paper.

## Input
- Problem: {{problem}}
- Proposed Method: {{method}}
- Target Venue: {{target_venue}}
- Domain: {{domain}}

## Requirements
1. Title should be 10-15 words maximum
2. Must include: (1) What is proposed, (2) What problem it solves, (3) Key innovation
3. Use active verbs: "Learning", "Optimizing", "Exploring", "Enhancing", etc.
4. Avoid generic terms like "Study", "Research", "Analysis" without specifics
5. For top venues (NeurIPS/ICML/ICLR), emphasize methodological novelty

## Output Format
Provide exactly 3 title options ranked by confidence:

Option 1 (Highest confidence): [Title]
Rationale: [Why this works best]

Option 2: [Title]
Rationale: [Why this is strong]

Option 3: [Title]
Rationale: [Alternative angle]

## Example Good Titles
- "Graph Contrastive Learning with Adaptive Augmentation"
- "Attention Is All You Need"
- "BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers"
- "Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition"
